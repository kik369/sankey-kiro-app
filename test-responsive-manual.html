Testing Instructions

1. Resize your browser window to test different breakpoints:

  • Mobile: < 640px - Single column layout, larger touch targets
  • Tablet: 640px - 1024px - Two column form, responsive grid
  • Desktop: > 1024px - Four column form, full layout

2. Check that:

  • Form inputs stack vertically on mobile, horizontally on desktop
  • Text sizes adjust appropriately (larger on mobile)
  • Touch targets are larger on mobile (py-2.5 vs py-2)
  • Statistics cards have background on mobile, transparent on desktop
  • Flow list items stack vertically on mobile
  • Buttons are full-width on mobile, auto-width on desktop
